Mistborn vs Misting

In the Final Empire, Allomancers swallow small amounts of metal and burn them internally. Mistings can burn a single metal, while Mistborn can burn all eight basic metals. Choose a path below to learn what each can do.

Mistborn

A Mistborn can burn all eight basic metals, combining their effects for fluid movement, heightened senses, emotional influence, and stealth. Mistborn are extremely rare in the story. Vin and Kelsier are prominent examples.
