Important locations

The story highlights a few places that show how the empire rules and where people suffer the most.

Luthadel

The capital city of the Final Empire, packed with noble mansions, skaa slums, and government ministries. It is the center of politics, wealth, and rebellion.

Kredik Shaw

The massive black palace and fortress of the Lord Ruler, looming over Luthadel as a symbol of absolute control.

Pits of Hathsin

A brutal mining prison where workers extract atium. Few prisoners survive long in the harsh conditions.
