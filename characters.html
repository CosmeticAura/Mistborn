Characters

The story follows a tight crew of thieves, scholars, and fighters whose skills and loyalties shape the rebellion.

Kelsier

Leader of the rebellion, known as the Survivor of Hathsin after escaping the empire's most brutal prison camp. He spreads hope among the skaa and pushes a plan to topple the Lord Ruler.

Vin

A street thief who discovers she is a Mistborn. She trains in Allomancy, learns to trust others, and slips into noble society for the crew.

Sazed

A Terris scholar and steward from a people known for their learning and service. He preserves knowledge of ancient religions and history while protecting the crew.

Dockson

The crew's strategist and organizer, responsible for planning operations and keeping the rebellion coordinated.

Breeze

A powerful Soother who manipulates emotions during social encounters and helps the crew move through noble circles.

Ham

A Pewterarm who serves as the crew's fighter, using pewter to boost strength and endurance in battle.

Clubs

A Smoker who hides Allomantic activity with a coppercloud, keeping the crew safe from Seekers.

Spook

A Tineye with extremely sharp senses who helps the crew scout and navigate the mists.
