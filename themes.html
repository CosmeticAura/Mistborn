Themes

The story keeps returning to hope, trust, oppression, and power as the rebellion grows.

Hope

Kelsier spreads hope among the skaa, using symbols and bold acts to push back against despair.

Trust

Vin learns to trust others after a lifetime of betrayal, and that growth becomes essential to the crew.

Oppression

The Final Empire is built on fear and control, and the skaa live under constant violence and restriction.

Power

The story looks at how magical and political power shape society, and how that power can be challenged.
