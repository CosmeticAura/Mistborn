The World of the Final Empire

The world feels harsh and unnatural, shaped by events in the distant past. Ash falls constantly, the sun is red, and the mists roll in every night.

Environmental features

The Final Empire is defined by constant ashfall from volcanoes, nightly mists, brown vegetation, and dark skies. Everything about the land feels altered and dangerous.

Red sun and ash

The sun appears red through the ash, and the air carries soot. Daylight never feels fully clear or clean.

Nightly mists

Mists come with the night and retreat by day. They cloak movement, conceal Allomancers, and feed the fear the empire relies on.

Social structure

The Lord Ruler's empire is rigidly divided between the nobility and the skaa.

Nobility

The ruling class descends from allies of the Lord Ruler. Nobles hold wealth, land, and influence, and many quietly possess Allomantic powers.

Skaa

The enslaved lower class works farms, factories, and mines. Skaa are forbidden from education and brutally punished for rebellion.
